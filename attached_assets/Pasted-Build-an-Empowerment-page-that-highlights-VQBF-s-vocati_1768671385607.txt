Build an Empowerment page that highlights VQBF’s vocational training programs for young girls and clearly communicates the journey from training to empowerment.

The page should be inspirational, informative, and visually rich, with dedicated sections for images and real impact stories.

Section 1: Page Hero

Create a hero section at the top of the page with:

Headline:
“Empowering Girls Through Skills, Opportunity, and Support”

Subtext:
“We equip young girls with practical vocational skills and empower them with the tools, resources, and confidence to build sustainable livelihoods.”

Hero Image Area:

Large image or image carousel

Images showing girls during training sessions (sewing, fashion design, hair making)

Optional overlay or soft gradient for text readability

Section 2: Our Empowerment Approach

Introduce how the empowerment program works in simple steps.

Section title:
“Our Empowerment Approach”

Display the steps in a horizontal flow or stacked cards:

Vocational Training
Young girls are trained in practical, income-generating skills.

Skill Development & Mentorship
Participants receive hands-on guidance, mentorship, and continuous assessment.

Completion & Evaluation
Trainees are evaluated upon successful completion of the program.

Empowerment & Support
Successful trainees are empowered with cash support, tools, materials, and starter items.

Visual support:

Icons or illustrations for each step

Optional supporting images under or beside the steps

Section 3: Vocational Training Programs

Highlight the specific vocational skills offered.

Section title:
“Vocational Training Programs”

Display programs as cards in a responsive grid.

Each program card should include:

Program image

Program title

Short description

Programs to display:

Sewing

Fashion Design

Hair Making / Hair Styling

Image space:

Each card must support a featured image

Images should show real training environments where possible

Section 4: Empowerment After Training

Explain what happens after successful completion.

Section title:
“Empowerment After Completion”

Content points:

Cash empowerment to support business startup

Provision of tools and materials (e.g., sewing machines, fabrics, hair kits)

Starter items to begin immediate income generation

Layout:

Text on one side

Image gallery or stacked images on the other side

Section 5: Impact & Success Stories

Showcase real outcomes from the program.

Section title:
“Impact & Success Stories”

Display testimonial or story cards with:

Trainee photo

Name (optional)

Training completed

Short success story or quote

Image support:

Before/after images (optional)

Training completion photos

Section 6: Image Gallery

Dedicated gallery section for visual storytelling.

Section title:
“Moments From Our Empowerment Programs”

Gallery requirements:

Grid or masonry layout

Supports multiple images

Lightbox or full-view on click (optional)

Optimized for mobile and desktop

Section 7: Call to Action

End the page with a strong CTA.

CTA headline:
“Join Us in Empowering the Next Generation”

CTA options:

Apply for a vocational training program

Partner or sponsor a trainee

Buttons:

Primary: Apply for Training

Secondary: Support the Program

General Design & UX Guidelines

Fully responsive across all screen sizes

Consistent spacing and typography

Subtle entry animations on scroll

Clear image placeholders where content is not yet available

Accessible text contrast and alt text for images

The page should clearly communicate training → growth → empowerment → impact, supported by strong visuals and human stories.
